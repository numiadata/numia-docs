NumiaEngage offers flexible earning models to suit various types of ad engagements, ensuring publishers can maximize their revenue:

- **Supported Earning Models**:
  - **CPM (Cost Per Mille)**: Earnings based on impressions.
  - **CPS (Cost Per Sale)**: Earnings based on completed sales through the ad.
  - **CPA (Cost Per Action)**: Earnings based on specific user actions, such as sign-ups or purchases.

- **Payment Schedule**: Payments are made **monthly** or **quarterly**, depending on the volume of earnings. This ensures a reliable payout structure tailored to individual performance levels.

With these options, you have the flexibility to choose the model that best aligns with their audience engagement and content type.
